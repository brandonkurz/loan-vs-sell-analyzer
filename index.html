Assumptions & Notes

Simplified planning illustration, not tax or investment advice. Tax at settlement is computed on the RSU income stacked on top of your other 2026 ordinary income through the federal brackets, plus state and Medicare/payroll — see the Income & Tax tab. Short-term gains (sold inside 12 months) are taxed at your marginal ordinary rate + 3.8% NIIT + state; long-term gains (held 12+ months) at the long-term federal rate + 3.8% NIIT + state. Borrowing keeps all shares; interest = loan × borrowing rate over the hold (simple, or compounding if capitalizing). Ignores AMT, capital-loss offsets below basis, dividends, and margin-call risk. Private-company shares aren't freely sellable — future prices are not guaranteed. Confirm with the client's CPA before acting.
